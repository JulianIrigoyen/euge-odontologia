# Google Business Profile — Unseen Alineadores

A Google Business Profile is the #1 way people in Recoleta will find you. When someone searches "alineadores invisibles Recoleta", the Maps listing with reviews, hours, and photos is what they see first.

---

## Setup (10 minutes)

### 1. Create the Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with the business Google account
3. Click **"Add your business"**
4. Business name: `Unseen Alineadores Invisibles`
5. Category: `Ortodoncista` (primary), add `Dentista` as secondary
6. Add the physical address (consultorio in Recoleta)
7. Service area: `Recoleta, Buenos Aires` + `Palermo` + `Belgrano` (nearby neighborhoods)

### 2. Verify Ownership

Google will verify you own the business. Options:
- **Postcard** (takes 5-7 days, mailed to the address)
- **Phone** (instant if available)
- **Email** (if you have a domain email)

### 3. Complete the Profile

After verification, fill in everything:

- **Phone:** +54 9 11 3179-3649
- **Website:** your Vercel URL (or custom domain once set up)
- **Hours:** consultorio hours
- **Description:** "Tratamiento de ortodoncia invisible con alineadores transparentes personalizados. Tecnología 3D, seguimiento profesional. Consultá sin compromiso. Mat. 42.671."
- **Photos:** Add at least 5:
  - The consultorio (exterior + interior)
  - Before/after cases
  - The aligners / packaging
  - The team
- **Services:** List each plan (Esencial, Completo, Premium)
- **Attributes:** Add "Acepta tarjeta de crédito", "Estacionamiento cercano", etc.

### 4. Get Reviews

Reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local search. After each patient finishes treatment:
1. Go to your Business Profile → **"Get more reviews"** → copy the link
2. Send the link via WhatsApp after their last appointment
3. Aim for 5+ reviews in the first month

### 5. Post Regularly

Google Business has a "Posts" feature (like mini social posts):
- Share before/after photos
- Announce promotions
- Post FAQs
- Google favors active profiles in search results

- Respond to every review (positive and negative) within 24 hours
- Add new photos monthly
- Keep hours updated (especially holidays)
- The profile links directly to WhatsApp if you set the phone number correctly
