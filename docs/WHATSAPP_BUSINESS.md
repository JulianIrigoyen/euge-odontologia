# WhatsApp Business Setup — Unseen Alineadores

All leads go to WhatsApp. Using WhatsApp Business (free) instead of regular WhatsApp adds auto-replies, business hours, and a catalog.

---

## Setup

### 1. Install WhatsApp Business

1. Download **WhatsApp Business** (green icon with a "B") from App Store / Play Store
2. Set it up with the business phone number: `+54 9 11 3179-3649`
3. You can use both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business on the same phone with different numbers, or migrate your existing number

### 2. Complete the Business Profile

In the app → **Settings** → **Business Tools** → **Business Profile**:

- **Business name:** Unseen Alineadores Invisibles
- **Category:** Dentist / Orthodontist
- **Description:** Alineadores invisibles personalizados en Recoleta. Consultá sin compromiso.
- **Address:** Recoleta, Buenos Aires
- **Hours:** your consultorio hours
- **Email:** unseenaligners@gmail.com
- **Website:** your Vercel URL

### 3. Set Up Auto-Replies

**Away Message** (when outside business hours):
> Settings → Business Tools → Away Message

```
¡Hola! Gracias por escribirnos 😊
En este momento estamos fuera de horario.
Te respondemos mañana a primera hora.

Horario de atención: Lun-Vie 9-18hs
```

**Greeting Message** (first time someone writes):
> Settings → Business Tools → Greeting Message

```
¡Hola! Bienvenido/a a Unseen Alineadores 😊
¿En qué podemos ayudarte?

1️⃣ Quiero una consulta inicial (gratis)
2️⃣ Quiero saber los precios
3️⃣ Tengo una consulta sobre mi tratamiento

Respondenos con el número y te ayudamos.
```

**Quick Replies** (saved responses you can send with a shortcut):
> Settings → Business Tools → Quick Replies

Set up shortcuts for common responses:
- `/precio` → pricing details
- `/turno` → how to schedule
- `/ubicacion` → address and how to get there
- `/post` → post-treatment care instructions

### 4. Labels

Organize chats with labels:
- 🟢 Consulta nueva
- 🟡 Presupuesto enviado
- 🔵 En tratamiento
- ✅ Tratamiento completo

## Tips

- Reply within 5 minutes during business hours (speed = conversion)
- Use the **Catalog** feature to show plans with prices and photos
- **Broadcast lists** for promotions (not groups — patients don't see each other)
- Link the WhatsApp Business number to Instagram for the "Message" button
